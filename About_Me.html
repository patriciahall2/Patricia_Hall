About Me

Hi. My name is Patricia Hall.

I am new to computer programming. I began taking coding classes at Epicodus at the beginning of January 2016.

I currently work at The Jasmine Pearl, a tea shop in Portland, Oregon where I am the editor of the shop's social media, blog, and newsletters. I decided to take coding classes because I want to make our web presense more impressive in terms of styling and be able to create webpages that are more interesting and user-friendly to our customers. I do not have a formal education in digital editing or marketing.

Outside of work I write and perform electronic music. I was a songwriter, synth player, and singer of a music project called Soft Metals from 2009 until 2015. In my free time I have been working on a new music project called Patricia et Cameron, which is currently in its writing stage.

I have toured extensively with Soft Metals. Traveling and sharing my music with others around the world has been one of the most rewarding experiences of my life. I hope to eventually incorporate coding in my music as well.
